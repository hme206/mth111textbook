Geometric Formulas
Rectangle
Perimeter   \(P = 2l + 2w\)
Area   \(A = lw\)
Square
Perimeter   \(P = 4s\)
Area   \(A = s^2\)
Circle
Perimeter   \(P = 2\pi r\)
Area   \(A = \pi r^2\)
where \(\pi \approx 3.14\) or \(\dfrac{22}{7}\)
Triangle
Perimeter   \(P = a + b + c\)
Area   \(A = \tfrac{1}{2}bh\)
\(m\angle A + m\angle B + m\angle C = 180°\)
Right Triangle
Pythagorean theorem
\(a^2 + b^2 = c^2\)
Parallelogram
Area   \(A = bh\)
Trapezoid
Area   \(A = \tfrac{1}{2}h(B + b)\)
Cube
Surface area   \(S = 6s^2\)
Volume   \(V = s^3\)
Rectangular Solid
Surface area   \(S = 2wh + 2lh + 2lw\)
Volume   \(V = lwh\)
Sphere
Surface area   \(S = 4\pi r^2\)
Volume   \(V = \tfrac{4}{3}\pi r^3\)
Right Circular Cylinder
Surface area   \(S = 2\pi r^2 + 2\pi rh\)
Volume   \(V = \pi r^2 h\)
Right Circular Cone
Surface area   \(S = \pi rl + \pi r^2\)
Volume   \(V = \tfrac{1}{3}\pi r^2 h\)
